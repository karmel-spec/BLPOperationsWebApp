Correction workflow

From mismatch to clean inventory

1Detect

Find missing fields, stale listings, mismatched statuses, price risks, and photo gaps.

2Compare

Look across Piano Log, Shopify, Shop Manager, Sales Console, and Accounting context.

3Recommend

Draft correction with source evidence, risk level, and suggested owner.

4Approve

Human approves price, public copy, availability, or major record changes.

5Sync

Update the correct source system and push approved public changes.

6Log

Save the audit trail and close the correction queue item.
