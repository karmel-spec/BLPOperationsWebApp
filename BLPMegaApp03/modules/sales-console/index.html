Sales Console Quick Reference

Action Column

Temp: 1-10 buying/restoration heat. 8+ means work today.

State: who has the ball. OUR TURN and FIRST CONTACT are highest urgency.

Last: arrow left means customer contacted us; arrow right means we contacted them.

Next: recommended channel, never shown when that contact method is missing.

Touches: BLP is outbound contact from our team; Client is inbound replies or client-initiated contact.

Daily Workflow

  1. Start with Inbox sorted by Temperature.
  2. Work OUR TURN, FIRST CONTACT, Hot, and Lonely leads before browsing the full list.
  3. Click Funnel boxes to load a focused work queue by timeline or aging.
  4. Open a lead drawer to edit Rep, Lead, Headline, Timeline, Action, email, or cell number.
  5. Review each AI draft before approving. Email approvals are designed to send from info@ once the backend is connected.
