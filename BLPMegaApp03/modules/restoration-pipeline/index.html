Restoration stages

The operating path from queue to delivery

Draft workflow
1Intake

Photos, customer story, piano identity, condition, and rough scope captured.

2Estimate

Scope, price range, timeline, parts, risks, and client-facing explanation prepared.

3Approved

Contract, selections, deposit, schedule, and owner/shop approval confirmed.

4Rebuild

Action, belly, refinishing, restringing, parts, and technician stage notes.

5Final prep

Regulation, tuning, voicing, polish, QC, photos, and delivery readiness.

6Delivery

Move plan, client confirmation, warranty record, follow-up, and final closeout.
